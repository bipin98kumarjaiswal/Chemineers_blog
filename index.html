WASTE TO ENERGY

Waste management has been a significant concern for the world for a long time.With the increase in waste generation in recent years,this concern has grown even more.Several devices and processes have been innovated to manage waste and use it for energy generation.These processes are known as Waste to Energy process.

PROCESS INTESIFICATION

Process intesification is the method in chemical engineering that enhances efficiency ,cost-effectiveness and eco-friendliness by boosting output,reducing energy consuption,maintaing quality,ensuring safety and minimizing waste.

WORLD WITHOUT CHEMICAL ENGINEERS

Chemical engineering, often overlooked, serves as more than just the cornerstone of traditional manufacturing; it emerges as a guiding light in the darkest of times, including the recent global pandemic. These professionals possess a unique blend of skills that extend far beyond mere equations and reactions. They are the trailblazers navigating complex processes, igniting innovation, and advocating for sustainability across diverse industries.
